About

This is an IoT Project based around home security, The goal of my product is to provide a detterent against intruders and/or the feeling of security.

Architecture Diagram

When completed there will be an ESP32 cam that will detect your face and if your face is in the database then you will be able to enter. If your face is not in the database, there will be an lcd screen that will promt the user to enter a pin, if you happen to get the pin wrong, then you will be sprayed with water for every time you get the pin wrong.
